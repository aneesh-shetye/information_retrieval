Lakhs of tourists visit Goa during the last few days of December, to ring in the new year. Inspector general of police (IGP) Jaspal Singh directed the district superintendents of police (SPs) to ensure that all security and law and order arrangements are made.Singh directed the SP-traffic to ensure that there is no obstructive parking on arterial roads causing traffic bottlenecks.The IGP further directed SP-special branch to deploy teams for collecting and transmitting information, in the face of any law and order situation that may occur during the revelry. Singh has instructed all deputy superintendents of police (DySP), police inspectors and traffic inspectors to be constantly available on their mobile phones.“No official mobile numbers should be diverted to personnel phones,” Singh warned. He instructed the deputy inspector generals of police (DIGs) and SPs to visit different areas and inspect the availability and the alertness of the deployed personnel .Arunachal PradeshSikkimArunachal
